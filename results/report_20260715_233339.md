## 7 · Big Local Pileup Benchmark (merged mode, single run)

One locus, 1000 distinct duplicate groups sharing a start position (40 duplicate reads each) — synthetic BAM from `generate_big_pileup_bam.py` (not committed, cached in `large_bam/`).
